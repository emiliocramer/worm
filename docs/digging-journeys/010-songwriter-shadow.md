# 010 — The Songwriter's Shadow (Lori McKenna)

Real digging story: the hit you love has a quieter, better-kept original owner.

"Humble and Kind" reached most people as a Tim McGraw single in January 2016.
It was written alone by Lori McKenna — a Massachusetts mother of five writing
a prayer for her kids — and six months later she released her own version on
The Bird and the Rifle (July 2016). The song took the Grammy for Best Country
Song and CMA Song of the Year as *her* song. Country and folk fans who follow
the writing credit off a radio hit routinely land in a songwriter's own
catalog: sparer, stranger, and usually deeper than the famous cut.

Journey shape:

```text
love for a written song (country, folk, singer-songwriter, americana)
-> the credit line under the famous name
-> the songwriter's own records and demos
-> the writer's version, and the album around it
```
