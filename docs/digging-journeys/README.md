# Digging Journeys

Source-backed routes into music.

This folder is for Worm's taste -> music problem: how a real person gets from a
mood, memory, obsession, place, price, sample, cover, scene, format, or trusted
person to a song or album that lands.

Do not summarize the source. Pull the journeys.

Derived notes:

- [hero-journeys.md](hero-journeys.md) turns repeated patterns from the source
  entries into candidate dig routes Worm could try against the brain.

Useful shape:

- What started the dig.
- Where they looked.
- What made them stop, skip, buy, stream, rip, save, or keep digging.
- What they found.
- What the find changed or opened next.

Rules:

- Stay inside the source.
- Keep the chain of cause visible.
- A source can have no usable journeys.
- Prefer specific routes over broad lessons.
- Keep side notes only when they explain the decision: price, rarity, format,
  era, label, credits, sound quality, social pressure, memory, place, availability.
