# 008 — Alias Worlds (Chino Moreno, Aphex Twin)

Real digging story: loving an artist hard enough that you follow the *person*
out the side door of the band.

Deftones fans who wore out White Pony found Chino Moreno's Team Sleep — a side
project started during the White Pony sessions whose album leaked in 2001 and
finally landed in 2005, after label interference that Moreno says made the
project "fade away." Later, Crosses (†††) songs "would mysteriously appear
online, with no further explanation," and fans pieced together it was Moreno
before any announcement. Palms added former Isis members. One devotion, four
catalogs.

The extreme case: Richard D. James has released as AFX, Polygon Window, Caustic
Window, Bradley Strider, GAK, The Tuss, and more — and in 2015 dumped 230+
unreleased tracks to SoundCloud as the anonymous accounts user48736353001 and
user18081971, adding more in 2020, 2023, and 2025. An Aphex Twin fan's dig is
never done; the alias tree keeps growing.

Journey shape:

```text
durable artist devotion (playlist named after them, years of plays)
-> the person behind the band
-> side projects, aliases, anonymous accounts, label-evasion drops
-> a catalog the fan never knew existed
```
