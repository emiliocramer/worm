# 009 — Producer Chains (Metro Boomin)

Real digging story: the name in the producer tag becomes the artist you dig.

Trap listeners who lived inside Future's DS2 and the 21 Savage/Offset tape
Without Warning kept meeting the same signature: Metro Boomin. Following the
credit instead of the rapper led to Not All Heroes Wear Capes (2018), Metro's
debut solo album as the headline artist — it went straight to number one — and
later Heroes & Villains (2022), where the producer curates Future, Young Thug,
and 21 Savage on his own record. The dig runs backward through beats the fan
already loves into albums filed under a name they never searched.

Journey shape:

```text
heavy rotation inside a producer-driven scene (trap, drill, grime, plugg)
-> the recurring producer behind the favorite tracks
-> that producer's own albums, curation projects, and instrumentals
-> a record the fan loves that never appears under the rapper's name
```
