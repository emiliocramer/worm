# 012 — Anonymous Drops (SAULT)

Real digging story: the best record of the year arrives with no name on it.

SAULT — helmed by producer Inflo with vocalist Cleo Sol — released their first
five albums without a single interview, live show, or music video. In November
2022 they surprise-dropped five albums at once as a free WeTransfer download,
available for five days. Acts of Faith arrived unannounced on Christmas Day
2024 as one 32-minute track. Finding SAULT was pure modern crate work: no
promo cycle, no algorithm placement, just listeners passing a link before it
expired. The same pattern covers Frank Ocean-style stealth drops and Burial's
years of anonymity — records that reward the listener who watches the corners
instead of the charts.

Journey shape:

```text
comfort with low-promo, low-popularity finds
-> releases that deliberately evade the promo machine
-> surprise drops, anonymous collectives, limited windows
-> the record everyone finds two years late, found on time
```
